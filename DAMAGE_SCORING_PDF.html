üö® CRITICAL: 10-8 & 10-7 GUARDRAILS

To award a 10-8 or 10-7 round, the winning fighter MUST meet at least ONE of these conditions:

  • 2 or more knockdown advantage over opponent
  • 100+ total strike differential (all strike types counted)

Without meeting these guardrails, the maximum score is 10-9, regardless of point differential.

This ensures 10-8 and 10-7 rounds are extremely rare and reflect true dominance or near-finish scenarios, aligned with professional MMA judging standards.

üéØ KEY PRINCIPLES

  • Damage > Volume: One near-finish moment is worth far more than many light strikes
  • Quality > Quantity: Significant strikes are worth 2√ó non-significant strikes
  • Balance Matters: 50/40/10 split means both striking and grappling can win rounds
  • 10-8 is Rare: Requires meeting specific guardrails (2+ KDs or 100+ strike differential)
  • Control Accumulates: Time-based control can add up to significant points over a round
  • Near-Finish Wins: Near-finish knockdowns or submissions almost always secure round victories
