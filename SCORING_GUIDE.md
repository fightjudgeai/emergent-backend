## ðŸŽ¯ KEY TAKEAWAYS

1. **Damage > Volume:** One Near-Finish KD (1.00) = Ten Significant Crosses (1.40)
2. **10-8 is RARE:** Need 2+ KDs or 100+ strike difference, not just dominance
3. **Balance Matters:** 50/40/10 split means striking slightly favored but grappling still very important
4. **Control Adds Up:** 3 minutes of back control (2.16 points) = 3 Hard Knockdowns (2.10 points)
5. **Quality > Quantity:** Significant strikes worth 2x non-significant strikes
6. **Near-Finish Moments:** Always win rounds unless opponent has them too
